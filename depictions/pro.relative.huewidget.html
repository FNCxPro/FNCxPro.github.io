Hue Widget

Compatible with iOS 9.0 to iOS 11.4

Description

Show a hue widget lightbulb on your lockscreen and homescreen
Works with Xen HTML
To get this working you have to...
1. Install the widget
2. Add it to your lock screen or home screen
3. Change the widget settings, change the field that asks you for the light names. Put a comma between each light name DO NOT PUT SPACES BETWEEN THE LIGHT NAMES AND COMMAS! 4. Press the link button on your physical Hue light bridge
5. Press the light bulb on the widget
6. You should be able to control your light bulbs!
