NoMoreNCTime

Compatible with iOS 11.0 to iOS 11.4

Description

Gets rid of that pesky date and time in the today section of the notification center. This tweak also removes the space that is created with Flex tweaks that do the same thing.

Method to hide date/time from sinfool's flex tweak, LS - Hide Time and Date (Customizable)
